AutoTrashBin

Due to poor waste treatment implementation, particularly the segregation of garbage inside the University of Antique, the Materials Recovery Facilities (MRFs) pickup vehicle was unable to collect waste. The "No Plastic Policy," which the University of Antique adopted, assisted in tackling the problem. In addition to that, me and my team came up with an additional strategy to enhance the campus's solid waste management system. The other is to use machine learning techniques to create the AutoTrashBin, an automatic waste segregator.

Rain and Flood Detector

This project is a Rain and flood alarm device that is essential in areas that are prone to heavy rainfall and flooding. These systems use microcontrollers Arduino Uno or Raspberry Pi and water sensor to detect water levels and alert people in the vicinity of potential dangers.

Ngutana

Ngutana is a Cebuano term for "asked". It is your virtual assistant where you can enter a specific topic or a mathematical computation that will give you the answer by utilizing the Wikipedia and Wolfram Alpha libraries, or using its API as an option. It also has text-to-speech features by using the pyttsx3 library to make this little project more interactive.
